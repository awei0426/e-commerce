Sprite

Sprite, Inc. is a Germany multinational corporation that is
engaged in the development and distribution
of lemon-flavored soda around the world.

Sprite is a clear, lemon and lime-flavored soft drink created by the Coca-Cola Company. Sprite comes in multiple flavors, including cranberry, cherry, grape, orange, tropical, ginger, and vanilla. Ice, peach, Berryclear remix, and newer versions of the drinks are artificially sweetened
